Our SERVICES

Nkuli Projects (Pty) Ltd is all about appeasing our clients.
We are well equipped and eager to make your idea a reality without you having to worry about time and budget. We also offer high quality products that both the client and ourselves are proud of. Our team of professional installers and handymen cover Floor & Wall Solutions such as tiling, laminates, vinyl, and ceiling solutions. With regards to General Plumbing & Maintenance, we cover sanitaryware installations, shower installations, kitchen sinks & tap installations, bathroom accessories and much, much more. We also deal with construction of residential and commercial buildings, renovations, painting services, and scaffold equipment rental We are a team dedicated to professional installation in accordance with the latest building codes and ethics of the South African National Standards. We cater to General Construction – residential (including home renovations & alterations), commercial and industrial services.

MEET THE TEAM

NATALIE FRIEDMAN

FOUNDER & MD

Natalie Friedman obtained her B-Tech degree in Quantity Surveying and is a Quantity Surveyor (QS) by profession. She studied at the Cape Peninsula University of Cape Town (CPUT) in 2003 – 2007. In 2013, she completed a course in Cost Control, whilst employed by a mining company in Johannesburg, South Africa. Natalie Friedman is the Founder and Managing Director. After 21 years of experience in the commercial, residential and industrial sites, her manifold knowledge has added the much needed drive for success in the business. As part of senior management, Natalie Friedman is responsible for the daily operations and commercial aspects of the business, keeping the business solvent and promoting expansion and innovation within the industry. Due to her strong beliefs, Friedman strives at improving the lives of people in our communities through infrastructure development. This also includes empowering women, alleviating poverty and contributing to the country’s Gross Development Product (GDP).


ZINZI LUKHANYO

CO-FOUNDER & MD

Zinzi Lukhanyo has a BA Marketing Communication degree which he obtained from AAA School of Advertising in Johannesburg. He is the Co-Founder & Managing Director. Lukhanyo has a vast amount of experience within the FMCG industries. He is adept in marketing management, digital strategy, project & event management and client relationship management. As a marketing and cooperate governance managing director, he is responsible for providing direction, guidance and strategic leadership within the organisation by managing the marketing and sales departments and leading the design and implementation of the organisation’s governance framework. This is to enable effective delivery of the company’s organisational policy objectives and optimised performance. Zinzi Lukhanyo maintains that as a society, we need to refocus our attention on creating an economy that works for everyone where businesses and communities work together in stimulating our country’s economic growth.
